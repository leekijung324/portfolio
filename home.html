About Me

I am a seasoned researcher with a PhD in Information Studies and over a decade of experience driving product strategy through a unique combination of rigorous user experience research and advanced data science. My work focuses on understanding complex user behaviors in digital environments, particularly in the realms of user privacy, AI-powered experiences, and online safety.

Throughout my career, I've led multi-stage research programs, published over 20 peer-reviewed papers, and developed graduate-level courses in Human-Computer Interaction and Database Management. I am passionate about translating complex data into actionable insights that inform design, influence product direction, and create better, safer digital platforms for everyone.
